About the Department

The Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) at IIUC is one of the leading engineering departments in Chittagong. Established with the vision of producing competent engineers who can contribute to national development, the department offers comprehensive programs in electrical power, electronics, telecommunications, and control systems.

Students receive hands-on training in well-equipped laboratories including Circuit Labs, Power Systems Lab, Electronics Lab, Communication Lab, and Microprocessor Lab. The curriculum is regularly updated to match industry standards and technological advancements.

Key Highlights

ABET-accredited curriculum, Industry partnerships with BPDB, DESCO, and telecom companies, Research opportunities in renewable energy and smart grid systems.
